## 3. Daily Learning Structure (Starting March 9, 2026)
Daily lessons delivered automatically early morning.
Complete in multiple short sessions during breaks.

### **Elements of the daily lesson:**
1. **今日の表現（Workplace Expression）**  
   - Natural keigo and business phrases.
2. **文法１つ（One Grammar Focus）**
   - JLPT N4→N3, optimized for workplace clarity.
3. **ミニ会話（Mini Dialogue）**
   - Respond as if speaking; corrections provided.
4. **リスニング風練習（Listening Simulation）**
   - Fast kana-style line → rewrite into proper Japanese.
5. **書く練習（Writing Practice）**
   - One or two sentences output based on grammar or workplace needs.
6. **MacWhisper Transcription Task（Light）**
   - Level B (one short sentence: 7–12 mora)
   - Workflow:
     1. Generate TTS with ElevenLabs  
     2. Check pitch in OJAD  
     3. Record your voice  
     4. Run MacWhisper  
     5. Paste transcription log  
     6. Optional: Upload audio + log to GitHub  
     7. Optional: Analyze with Gemini

Difficulty will scale with improvement.

## 5. Sentence Mining System

### **1T (One-Target) sentence rule:**
Every card introduces just one new:
- word,  
- grammar pattern,  
- reading, or  
- nuance.

### **Mining Sources**
- Work emails (highest value)
- 聖書 新共同訳 (formal + structured)
- N3 podcasts (Let’s Talk in Japanese, Teppei)
- Anime (1–2 episodes/week for natural rhythm)

### **Card Content**
- Sentence text  
- ElevenLabs audio  
- OJAD pitch curve  
- Conversational short example  
- Your voice audio  
- MacWhisper logs  
- Source context  

---

## 6. Audio Pipeline (Daily or Every Other Day)

### **Step 1: ElevenLabs**
Generate clear, natural Japanese audio.

### **Step 2: OJAD (Suzuki-kun)**
Visualize pitch accent:
- pitch drops  
- accent nucleus  
- mora timing  

### **Step 3: Self Recording**
Match:
- pitch  
- rhythm  
- mora pacing  
- consistent vowels  

### **Step 4: Whisper Transcription**
Use MacWhisper:
- check for missing vowels  
- check mora contraction  
- validate kana sequence  
- identify pitch drift (via mis-heard particles)

### **Step 5: GitHub Logging (optional)**
Upload:
- your audio  
- the ElevenLabs reference  
- Whisper text output  

### **Step 6: Gemini Analysis (optional)**
Analyze:
- vowel reduction  
- devoicing  
- rhythm inaccuracies  
- sentence-level prosody
